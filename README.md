I was born in Kharkiv, Ukraine. My dad was an executive working for the Soviet space program, and my mom was a civil engineer. 


At the age of 9 I was lucky to get a really good math teacher, a girl just out of college, that kempt feeding me fun math problems, to solve. This ended up determining my entire life.

I ended up spending my school years participating in different math and physics competitions and reading [books](https://www.amazon.com/Surely-Youre-Joking-Mr-Feynman-ebook/dp/B003V1WXKU/ref=sr_1_1?crid=6BKKV3YNY9B2&dchild=1&keywords=richard+feynman&qid=1627658417&s=digital-text&sprefix=richard+fey%2Cdigital-text%2C285&sr=1-1) of Richard Feynman
